Beauty Care

At Mea’s Aesthetics, beauty goes beyond the lash table — it’s about preserving the health of your natural lashes. We emphasize proper application, gentle handling, and clean products for stunning results that last. Your aftercare matters: avoid water, steam, and oil-based products in the first 24 hours after your appointment. For longer retention, brush your lashes daily, avoid rubbing your eyes, and schedule regular infills every 2–3 weeks.
