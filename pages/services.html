Our Services

Lash Extension Services

Enhance your natural beauty with our premium lash sets tailored to your style.

  • Classic Set: One lash extension per natural lash for an elegant look.
  • Hybrid Set: Blend of classic and volume for soft fullness.
  • Volume Set: Fluffy and dramatic using multiple fine lashes.
  • Mega Volume Set: Ultra-bold, full glam with maximum density.
  • Fox Eye Set: Lifted and elongated lashes for a sultry look.
  • Wispy Set: Textured and fluttery with varied lash lengths.
  • Wet Look Set: Sleek and bold like a fresh mascara coat.

Extras & Aftercare

  • Lash Removal: Gentle removal that protects your natural lashes.
  • Lash Bath: Deep cleanse to keep lashes healthy and buildup-free.
  • Patch Test: Allergy testing done 24–48 hours before first set.
  • Refills (2–3 weeks): Maintain fullness and shape regularly.
  • Lash Aftercare Kit: Cleanser, brush & care guide included.
